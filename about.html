About the Nexus

This site is a hub for Star Wars fans to explore the universe's factions, characters, and planets. The website was designed by University of South Carolina student Josh Doyon. The initial inspiration for the site was to complete an assignment for my ITEC362 class, but I wanted the site to help scratch my creative itch for the movie series I enjoy the most.

Ever since I was a kid, I've been captivated by the Star Wars universe. From the first time I watched the original trilogy with my Dad, I was hooked on the epic battles, rich storytelling, and unforgettable characters. Growing up, I loved everything Star Wars, from the movies and shows to books and games. It sparked my imagination and fostered a lifelong passion for sci-fi, and I am very excited to see how the website ends up!
