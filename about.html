About the Nexus

This website was created for a project for my ITEC362 class at the University of South Carolina. The website was designed to serve as a space for star wars fans, both casual and extreme, to have a place to learn about anything in the star wars universe!

My name is Josh Doyon, and I am currently an undergrad student at the University of South Carolina. I am studying integrated information technology, and I plan to pursue a career in large-scale commercial IT. I have been a star wars fan for as long as i can remember, and I am very excited to work on this project!
