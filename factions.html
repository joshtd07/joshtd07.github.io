Factions

The Galactic Empire An era of political power, where the Galactic Republic governed, and the Sith Empire often rose to challenge it. Major wars between Jedi and Sith defined this time, leading to the eventual fall of the Sith and the Republic's reformation.
The Rebel Alliance A coalition of freedom fighters opposing the Galactic Empire, aiming to restore democracy and overthrow imperial tyranny, leading to the Empire's defeat.
The Galactic Republic A democratic union of planets governed by the Senate and protected by the Jedi, it existed for millennia before falling to the Sith and transforming into the Empire.
The Confederacy of Independent Systems A separatist alliance of star systems led by Count Dooku, fighting against the Republic during the Clone Wars with a vast droid army.
