Characters

Darth Vader Once Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker, he fell to the dark side and became a Sith Lord under Emperor Palpatine. Vader is central to the fall of the Jedi, the rise of the Empire, and the eventual redemption arc through his son, Luke.
Luke Skywalker The son of Anakin Skywalker/Darth Vader and Padmé Amidala, Luke is the central hero of the original trilogy. He trains as a Jedi, confronts Darth Vader, and helps overthrow the Empire, restoring balance to the Force.
Captain Rex A loyal and skilled clone trooper, Rex serves under Jedi General Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. He leads the 501st Legion, becomes a trusted ally of Ahsoka Tano, and resists Order 66. Rex later joins the Rebel Alliance, playing a key role in the fight against the Empire.
Poe Dameron A daring and skilled pilot in the Resistance, Poe flies with the squadron against the First Order. He becomes one of General Leia Organa’s most trusted leaders, playing key roles in major battles and helping to defeat the Sith Eternal in The Rise of Skywalker.
