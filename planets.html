Planets

Tatooine A desert planet home to moisture farmers, smugglers, and crime lords. It is also the birthplace of Anakin and Luke Skywalker.
Naboo A lush, peaceful planet known for its royal monarchy and Gungan inhabitants. It’s the home of Padmé Amidala and Lord Palpatine, and the site of multiple major events in The Phantom Menace.
Hoth An icy, desolate world where the Rebel Alliance established Echo Base. It’s famous for the Battle of Hoth, an iconic clash in The Empire Strikes Back.
Mustafar A volcanic planet where Anakin Skywalker, while turning to Darth Vader, battles Obi-Wan Kenobi. It’s here that Anakin is gravely injured, marking his full turn to the dark side in Revenge of the Sith.
